Our research centers and institutes are where new discoveries happen and inform the latest approaches to medicine, health, and patient care. From shedding light on the implications of health care policy to uncovering the basic mechanisms that fuel cancer, RBHS centers and institutes break new ground.

Research Centers and Institutes

Primary Rutgers Biomedical and Health Sciences research centers and institutes include the following:

  • Cancer Institute of New Jersey
  • Center for Advanced Biotechnology and Medicine
  • Environmental and Occupational Health Sciences Institute
  • Institute for Health, Health Care Policy, and Aging Research

Many other RBHS centers and institutes are based in individual RBHS schools and at University Behavioral Health Care.

Rutgers also is home to hundreds of additional centers and institutes that work collaboratively across disciplines with RBHS, with other Rutgers units, and with other institutions worldwide.
